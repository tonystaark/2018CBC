SENIOR PASTOR’S MESSAGE

With my warm greetings in the most precious Name of our Lord Jesus Christ! As I write for this issue of CBC News, we had just returned from our Vision Casting Sunday on 28 January 2018. I trust that the Spirit of God fill our heart with great excitement and anticipation as we outwork the vision of ‘A Year of Bearing Fruit’ in our local church, corporately, and as each of us internalize it, apply it and work it out in our personal life, individually.
